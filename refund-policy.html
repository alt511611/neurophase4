Refund Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

30-day guarantee

If you purchase Neurophase 4 (or the Sleep & Recovery add-on) and don't notice a difference within 30 days of your purchase date, email support@neurophase4.com and we'll issue a full refund. No refund request form, and we won't ask you to justify it beyond a simple "it didn't work for me."

How the refund is issued

Refunds are processed back to your original payment method through Whop. Depending on your bank, it may take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement after we approve it.

What happens to access

Once a refund is issued, your dashboard access to the paid files is revoked.

Time window

The 30-day window is measured from the date of purchase. Requests made after 30 days will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, but are not guaranteed.
